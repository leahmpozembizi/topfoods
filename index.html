My Top 5 Kenyan Eats

These is my favourite foods from Kenya. I like them a lot and they make me feel like home.

  1. #1

    Ugali

    Ugali is plain and filling. I eat it with sauce and it feels good.

  2. #2

    Nyama Choma

    Grilled meat is my favorite when I am with friends. It tastes strong and nice.

  3. #3

    Sukuma Wiki

    Green leaves cooked with onions. It keeps me healthy and I like it.

  4. #4

    Chapati

    Chapati is soft and warm. I eat it with beans and it is good.

  5. #5

    Mandazi

    Sweet bread is easy to eat with tea. It are small and tasty.
